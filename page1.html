Learn how to make a Strawberry Cake🍰!

Ingredients

  • fresh strawberries🍓
  • flower
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • sugar🍬
  • butter🧈
  • 2 whole eggs🥚
  • 4 egg white
  • vanilla extract🌿
  • cream🧁

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside
  3. In a large bowl with a stand or hand mixer, cream the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the egg whites, mixing until just combined. Then add the whole eggs and mix again. Add the vanilla.
  5. Alternately add the flour mixture and milk, mixing until just combined. Gently fold in the remaining strawberry purée.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool the cake completely and add cream on it.
  8. Garnish with fresh strawberries and chill.
